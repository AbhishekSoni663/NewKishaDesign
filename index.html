How we do what we do

OUR FREE CONSULTATION CALL
Start your styling journey with a free consultation call. We will explain our process, services, packages, and pricing. This is also an opportunity for us to understand your requirements, budget and event details.
ONE-ON-ONE STYLE CONSULTATION
After your initial call, you will now have a personalized session with our head stylist, Noopur. She will evaluate your physique, personal style and event needs to recommend clothing and accessories that best fit your personality and the occasion. Together, we finalize favourites worthy of your wedding day, and everything in between.
OUTFIT AND ACCESSORIES CURATION
Collaborate with the stylist to select outfits and accessories for each ceremony, ensuring a cohesive look that aligns with your moodboards. We love infusing personal elements into your wedding attire that reflect your unique style.
SETTING IN PERSON APPOINTMENTS
Arrange meetings with vendors and designers to finalize outfits, jewellery, and accessories and discuss bespoke options.
COORDINATION AND DELIVERY
The Shaadi Stylist manages all coordination, including scheduling fitting appointments, handling alterations and customizations, so you can relax and focus on your love story, knowing your outfits are in good hands.
